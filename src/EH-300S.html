EH-300S

The EH-300S is a high-performance hot runner controller that is specifically designed for use in a wide variety of industries. This controller features advanced technology and intuitive controls, making it easy to set up and operate. It is capable of controlling up to 24 zones, making it perfect for small to medium-sized hot runner systems. It also has built-in safety features, such as over-temperature protection, to ensure safe and reliable operation.
